# Product Context

## Purpose
* Help job seekers find jobs fast.
* Provide clear market trend analytics (salary, skills, regions).
* Answer career questions via AI chatbot.

## Target Audience
* Job seekers (tech, general).
* Market analysts.

## UX Guidelines
* Clean, ultra-premium interface.
* Modern typography, vibrant colors.
* Glassmorphism, smooth animations.
* Responsive layouts (desktop and mobile).
